Good evening. In Willowbrook, a symbolic vote has drawn unexpected attention. Residents gathered today to debate a proposal to rename the town’s public library “The Great Repository of Unsolicited Advice.” City councilmembers said the idea originated from a neighborhood petition that gained rapid momentum.

(V.O.)
Supporters argued the new name would reflect the library’s role as a community hub for guidance and shared experience. Opponents dismissed the effort as unnecessary and distracting from core library services. After extended discussion, the council voted to keep the original name, while designating a small section of the building as a space for community advice events.

(ON-CAMERA)
Officials say the decision preserves tradition while acknowledging public interest. No operational changes are expected. More updates as this story develops.
